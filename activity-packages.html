Lunar Activities

ZERO G BASKETBALL

Come partake in our dome's athletics center's pick up space basketball games with only a fifth of earth's gravity to contend against!

MOON BUGGY

Take part in a one of a kind moon rally. After being given a thorough one hour brief, spend the next hour exploring the moon like the pioneer you are!

MOON CHEESE

During exploration, we realized that cheese left in a vaccum developed a very unique taste. The rest... is history!

JOINING THE DARK SIDE

The dark side of the moon has been hidden from humanity until now! Embark on our shuttle to explore it for yourself!

STORMTROOPER ACADEMY

Join this tongue-in-cheek recreation of a stormtrooper academy is faithfully recreated from the Star Wars franchise. Your guns are guarranteed to miss their mark!

MOON DOME

The "DOME" is the climate controlled bubble you will be living in for the duration of your trip. Join-us for a guided tour of its history.

MOON WEDDING

A reception. A banquet. A ceremony. A story as old as time. Book your special day with us as part of the wedding package, or attend wedding rehearsal that are ongoing through the month. An unforgettable experience!

GREENHOUSE LUNA

Every weekend, get the chance to visit LUNA, the moon's first every greenhouse, and help our botanists grow lichen used in our terraforming projects!

GIFT SHOP

Immortalize your moon stay by buying a replica of Apollo 14's command module made with actual lunar ore!

NOVA LUNA

Terraforming the moon and creating a permanent atmosphere is the engineering challenge of a millenia, and the endeavour of generations. Come plant with us O2 producing lichen and be immortalized in history.

SPACE WALK

Sign your liability waiver and hop in a TCSS, that is, Thrust Controlled Spacesuit. Each day you will partake in 3 hours of rigourous exercises and drills in preparation for an unassisted spacewalk on the flight home!

MOON ROCK

Canadian Astronaut extraordinaire Chris Hadfiled has signed a two-year residency at the New LUNA hotel. We guaranteed you haven't lived until you've listened to "Major Tom" while looking down on the earth.
